# Brand strategy — Material Memory Studio

Concise reference for positioning, audience, tone, and current structure.

## Positioning

- **What:** Private research studio and salon in Seoul at the intersection of art, coaching, and material research. Not a single discipline; intentional, gradual, by appointment.
- **Feel:** Held space for serious inquiry — not a product or mass offering. Confident naming what is "in development" or "upcoming."

## Audience

- People seeking reflective coaching (especially Border Crossing Sessions).
- Artists, designers, researchers, founders, practitioners interested in studio offerings, works, or dialogue.
- Visitors who want to follow the studio (Instagram) or read coaching/reflections (whywhatmatters.com). People drawn to careful thinking and conversation that resists easy resolution.

## Tone

- **Elegant, restrained, reflective.** No hype, no hard sell.
- **Precise** — "By appointment", "Saturdays", "in-person in Seoul", "limited pilot" over vague promises.
- **Warm but not casual** — For people drawn to depth.
- **Avoid:** Overly commercial language; startup clichés; making AI the center (AI is a supporting tool); corporate or generic buzzwords; over-translated or stiff Korean.

## Current pillars

1. **Artist Studio** — Jay Lee’s practice: materials, prints, research, works.
2. **Salon** — Conversation platform: visiting speakers, gatherings, talks.
3. **Coaching** — Border Crossing Sessions and transitional inquiry.
4. **Material Research** — Material archive, biomaterial exploration, regional materials.

## Current offerings (summary)

- **Border Crossing Sessions** — Distinct coaching format; BORDER Framework™; in-person Seoul, Saturdays, 60/90 min; pilot. Main bookable offering.
- **Studio Conversations** — Intimate, appointment-based dialogue; not consultation.
- **Original works & art prints** — From studio practice and nomadic archive; jayartmaking.com/works for archive.
- **Material Research Archive** — In development.
- **Material-led workshops** — Upcoming (e.g. from April).
- **Future salons & guest hosts** — Upcoming (e.g. from May).
